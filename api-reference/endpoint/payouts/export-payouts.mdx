---
title: "Export Payouts"
api: "GET /payout/export"
---

### 📝 Summary

This endpoint allows the export of payout data, typically used for generating reports or downloading payout records in bulk. The data can be filtered by date range, payout status, or other relevant parameters, and is typically exported in CSV or Excel format.


---

#### Body Parameters
<Expandable>
